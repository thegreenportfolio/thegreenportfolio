The Green Portfolio – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

The Green Portfolio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) builds privacy-first mobile apps and websites focused on movement, play, learning, local events, and practical work tools. Respect for your privacy is a core design principle across all our products.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:
• All apps published by The Green Portfolio
• All websites and subdomains operated by The Green Portfolio

This includes Bike Ride Green, Event Radar, JobConnect, GOAL! GOAL! GOAL!, Trivia Maze, Labirintul Școlar, and other apps published by The Green Portfolio.

3. Personal Data

Many of our apps are designed to work without accounts. Some apps, such as JobConnect, require an account to provide their core functionality. Other apps, such as Bike Ride Green, may offer optional accounts for online summaries, private groups, clubs, or challenges.

When an account is required or optional, we collect only the information needed to provide that service, such as login details, profile information, job or company details, preferences, saved records, matches, requests, unlock status, or uploaded summaries.

We do not sell personal information.

4. App Data

Some app data is stored locally on your device, such as saved events, game progress, ride history, scores, settings, or preferences.

For apps with account features, some data may be stored securely online so the app can provide its service. For example, JobConnect stores account, job seeker, employer, job post, match, request, and unlock information needed for matching. Bike Ride Green may store selected uploaded ride summaries if you choose to use online account features.

You may delete local app data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. For account-based features, deletion options may be available inside the app or by contacting support.

5. Location Data

Some apps may request access to location services to enable core functionality (for example, route tracking or event search by selected location). Location data is used only for the feature you choose to use. Bike Ride Green uses location during active rides for route tracking. Event Radar uses selected country and city search details to find events.

6. Website Analytics

Our websites may use basic analytics or advertising tools (such as Meta Pixel) to understand general traffic patterns and improve visibility. These tools do not provide us with personally identifiable information.

7. Third-Party Services

Some apps published by The Green Portfolio may use third-party services to provide specific functionality, such as advertising, analytics related to advertising performance, or mapping features.

These services are used only where necessary and in accordance with their respective privacy policies. We do not sell user data, and we do not share personally identifiable information with third parties.

Data shared with third-party services is limited to what is technically required for the feature to function (for example, ad delivery or map rendering) and is not used by us for profiling or tracking across apps.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Apps that are intended for general audiences are designed to minimize personal data collection. Apps with account-based features should be used only by users who are old enough to create an account under the applicable app rules and laws.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share personal information except where needed to provide app functionality, comply with legal requirements, protect our services, or operate trusted third-party services used by the app.

In JobConnect, contact details may be shared between an employer and a job seeker only through the app’s approval and unlock flow.

If you choose to export data from an app, that action is fully under your control.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if our products or legal requirements change. Updates will be published on this page with a revised date.
